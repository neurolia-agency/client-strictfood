# Few-Shot Examples

Real input→prompt transformations organized by model and category.

## GPT Images Examples

### 1. Food — Simple Request
**User**: "A photo of pasta"
**Prompt**:
```
A 45-degree close-up photograph of a plate of fresh tagliatelle tossed in a rich
bolognese sauce, with shaved Parmigiano-Reggiano melting on top. A few basil leaves
placed casually. The pasta glistens with olive oil. Served on a rustic cream ceramic
plate on a dark wooden table. Soft side-light from the left, warm golden tones.
Shot with an 85mm lens, shallow depth of field. No text, no watermark.
```

### 2. Food — With Text
**User**: "Menu image for a crêpe with the name of the dish"
**Prompt**:
```
A top-down photograph of a French crêpe filled with Nutella and sliced strawberries,
folded in quarters on a white plate. Powdered sugar dusted on top, a drizzle of
chocolate sauce. Clean white background. Soft, even overhead lighting.
The text "CRÊPE NUTELLA" in elegant uppercase serif font displayed below the plate
in dark brown. Minimalist menu card aesthetic. Square format.
```

### 3. Product — E-commerce

```

### 4. Portrait — Lifestyle

### 8. Abstract — Texture

## Gemini (Nano Banana Pro) Examples

### 9. Food — Dessert
**User**: "A photo of a waffle"
**Prompt**:
```
A golden Belgian waffle sits on a slate plate, its deep grid pattern perfectly
caramelized to a warm amber. A generous scoop of vanilla ice cream is beginning
to melt into the waffle's crevices, creating small rivers of cream. Fresh
strawberries are sliced and fanned beside the waffle, their bright red contrasting
with the golden pastry. A light dusting of powdered sugar catches the soft
side-light coming from a nearby window. The scene is captured at a 45-degree angle
with a 50mm lens, the background dissolving into a warm bokeh of a cozy café
interior. The mood is indulgent and comforting, with warm honey and cream tones
throughout the image.
```

### 10. Food — Savory

### 11. Product — Cosmetics

### 12. Portrait — Environmental
**User**: "Photo of a chef"
**Prompt**:
```
A chef stands in their kitchen, hands resting confidently on a stainless steel
counter, a gentle smile suggesting pride in their craft. They wear a well-worn
white chef's coat with the sleeves rolled to the elbows, revealing forearms that
speak to years of hard work. Behind them, the kitchen is alive but soft: copper
pans hang from a rack, and the warm glow of a gas flame flickers in the distance,

### 13. Landscape — Travel
**User**: "Sunset beach photo"
**Prompt**:
```
The last light of day paints the sky in layers of warm peach, deep coral, and
fading violet above a tranquil Mediterranean beach. The wet sand reflects the
sky's colors like a natural mirror, with gentle waves lapping at the shoreline
leaving delicate foam patterns. A lone weathered wooden boat rests on the sand
in the middle distance, adding a focal point and sense of timelessness. The sun
sits just above the horizon, casting a brilliant golden path across the calm water.
In the foreground, scattered seashells and smooth pebbles add texture and depth.
Shot at 24mm to capture the expansive sky, f/11 for sharpness from foreground to
horizon. The overall feeling is peaceful, nostalgic, and deeply warm.
```

### 14. Food — Beverage

browns and cool greens, creating a refreshing summer atmosphere.
