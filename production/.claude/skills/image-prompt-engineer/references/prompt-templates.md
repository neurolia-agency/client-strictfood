# Prompt Templates

Fill-in-the-blank templates for common image categories. Replace `[bracketed]` sections with specifics.

---

## Food Photography

```
A [angle: overhead/45°/straight-on] [shot type: close-up/medium/wide] of
[dish: full description with textures and colors].
[Plating: arrangement, garnish, sauce placement].
Served in/on [vessel: plate type, material, color].
[Props: surface, utensils, accompaniments, textiles].
[Lighting: type + direction], creating [shadow/highlight effect].
Shot with a [focal length]mm lens at f/[aperture].
[Mood: color temperature + atmosphere + season].
```

### Filled Example
```
A 45-degree close-up of a golden-brown croque monsieur with melted gruyère
bubbling on top, béchamel sauce dripping down the crusty edges.
Diagonally placed on a white ceramic plate with a small side salad of frisée.
On a weathered bistro table with a checkered napkin and a café crème in the background.
Soft window light from the left, creating gentle shadows on the right side.
Shot with an 85mm lens at f/2.8.
Warm, inviting Parisian lunch atmosphere.
```

## Product Shot

```
A [style: commercial/editorial/lifestyle] photograph of [product: detailed description].
[Position: centered/angled/floating/in-use].
[Surface/Background: material, color, texture or gradient].
[Supporting elements: complementary objects, ingredients, brand elements].
[Lighting: studio setup or natural, number of sources, direction].
[Composition: framing, negative space, depth].
[Technical: aspect ratio, any text to include].
```

### Filled Example
```
A commercial product photograph of an artisan ceramic coffee mug in warm terracotta
with a matte finish and organic irregular rim.
Slightly angled to show both the handle and interior.
On a raw concrete surface with a soft charcoal gradient background.
A few whole coffee beans scattered nearby and a single green eucalyptus sprig.
Three-point studio lighting: key light from upper-left, fill from right, rim light from behind.
Centered composition with generous negative space on the right for copy placement.
Square 1:1 aspect ratio for e-commerce listing.
```

## Portrait / Lifestyle

```
A [style: editorial/candid/environmental/studio] portrait of [subject description].
[Action/Pose: what they're doing, expression, body language].
[Setting: location, background elements, context].
[Wardrobe: clothing, accessories, colors].
[Lighting: type, direction, mood it creates].
[Camera: lens, angle, depth of field].
[Color palette and emotional tone].
```

### Filled Example
```
An editorial portrait of a young barista in a linen apron, mid-motion pouring
steamed milk into a ceramic cup, focused expression with a slight smile.
Behind the counter of a minimalist specialty coffee shop, shelves of kraft bags
and glass jars softly blurred in the background.
Wearing a simple black t-shirt under a natural beige apron, sleeves rolled up.
Warm window light from the left illuminating their hands and the pour, with a
gentle fill bounce from the right.
Shot at 85mm f/1.8, eye-level, shallow depth of field isolating the subject.
Warm earth tones, authentic craftsmanship mood.
```

## Architecture / Interior

```
A [style: architectural/interior design/real estate] photograph of [space description].
[Key architectural features: materials, shapes, focal points].
[Furnishing/Décor: furniture, art, plants, textiles].
[Lighting: natural light sources, artificial accent lighting, time of day].
[Composition: angle, leading lines, symmetry, vertical correction].
[Atmosphere: mood, season, occupancy (empty/lived-in)].
[Technical: wide-angle lens, vertical lines straight].
```

### Filled Example
```
An interior design photograph of a Scandinavian-inspired living room with
double-height ceilings and floor-to-ceiling windows overlooking a forest.
Exposed light wood beams, polished concrete floor with a large wool area rug.
A low-profile modular sofa in warm grey, a walnut coffee table with a few books
and a ceramic vase with dried pampas grass. A reading nook by the window with
a sheepskin-draped armchair.
Late afternoon golden light streaming through the windows, casting long warm
shadows across the floor. A single pendant light glowing softly above.
Shot at 24mm from a corner, using the sofa and windows as leading lines toward
the forest view. Vertical lines kept perfectly straight.
Calm, hygge atmosphere, lived-in but curated.
```

## Branding / Social Media

```
A [format: Instagram post/story/banner/thumbnail] image for [brand/purpose].
[Visual concept: what the image communicates].
[Main elements: product, person, graphic elements].
[Brand colors: specific hex or descriptions].
[Text overlay space: where to leave room for copy].
[Style: minimalist/bold/playful/elegant/rustic].
[Mood and target audience].
```

### Filled Example
```
An Instagram post image for a specialty coffee brand announcement.
Visual concept: the craft of coffee preparation as ritual.
A pair of hands in a dark apron carefully arranging a pour-over setup:
a ceramic dripper on a glass carafe, a gooseneck kettle pouring in a slow spiral.
Brand colors: warm cream background, deep brown tones, touches of terracotta.
Left third of the image kept as clean negative space for text overlay.
Minimal, warm, artisanal style with soft natural lighting.
Targeting young professionals who appreciate slow, intentional living.
```

## Usage Notes

- These templates are starting points — adapt elements based on the specific request
- Not every field needs filling — omit elements that don't apply
- For Gemini: convert the template output into flowing narrative paragraphs
- For GPT Images: the structured format works well as-is
- Always consider the final use case when deciding level of detail
