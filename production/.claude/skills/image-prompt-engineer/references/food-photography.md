# Food Photography — Prompt Engineering Guide

## The 5 Pillars of Food Photography

### 1. Texture
The most important differentiator. Food must look like you can taste it.

**Texture Vocabulary:**
| Category | Descriptors |
|----------|-------------|
| **Crispy/Crunchy** | golden-brown crust, shatteringly crisp, crackled surface, flaky layers |
| **Creamy/Smooth** | velvety sauce, silky ganache, smooth custard, whipped peaks |
| **Glossy/Wet** | glistening glaze, dewy fresh, lacquered surface, oil-slicked |
| **Steaming/Hot** | wisps of steam rising, heat shimmer, bubbling edges |
| **Melting** | stretching cheese, pooling butter, softening ice cream, dripping |
| **Fresh/Raw** | crisp leaves, water droplets, vibrant color, turgid vegetables |
| **Caramelized** | deep amber, burnished edges, sticky-sweet surface, Maillard crust |
| **Crumbly** | rustic crumbs scattered, tender flaky interior, rough-hewn edges |
| **Frozen/Cold** | frost crystals, condensation beads, icy glisten, frozen drip |

### 2. Plating & Composition
- **Intentional but not fussy**: "Artfully arranged but with a casual, just-served feel"
- **Negative space**: Leave room on the plate — not every inch needs filling
- **Height**: Build upward for visual interest — "stacked", "layered", "piled"
- **Garnish with purpose**: "A single sprig of fresh thyme" not "decorated with herbs"
- **Sauce work**: "A swoosh of reduction on the plate", "sauce pooling naturally beside"

### 3. Props & Styling
Think about what surrounds the food:
- **Surface**: Weathered wood, cool marble, slate, linen, concrete, ceramic tile
- **Utensils**: Vintage silverware, rustic wooden spoon, chopsticks, bread knife mid-cut
- **Accompaniments**: Scattered ingredients, fresh herbs, whole spices, bread torn not cut
- **Hands**: "Hands reaching for a slice" adds human connection and scale
- **Napkins/Textiles**: Rumpled linen, striped kitchen towel, folded cloth napkin

### 4. Lighting
Food photography lives and dies by lighting.

| Light Type | Effect | Best For |
|-----------|--------|----------|
| **Soft window light (side)** | Natural, editorial, clean | Almost everything — default choice |
| **Backlight** | Rim glow on steam/drinks, dramatic | Beverages, soups, steamy dishes |
| **45° side light** | Texture emphasis, natural shadows | Textured foods (bread, pastry) |
| **Overhead diffused** | Even, flat, modern | Flat lays, spreads, grazing tables |
| **Warm golden hour** | Cozy, nostalgic, appetite-inducing | Comfort food, autumn dishes |
| **Hard direct light** | Bold shadows, Mediterranean feel | Summer foods, fruit, outdoor dining |
| **Candlelight/ambient** | Intimate, moody, date-night | Fine dining, wine, desserts |

**Key rule**: Always specify light direction AND quality: "Soft side-light from the left" not just "good lighting".

### 5. Camera Angle

| Angle | Best For | Avoid For |
|-------|----------|-----------|
| **Overhead (90°)** | Flat lays, pizza, salads, spreads | Tall foods (burgers, drinks, cakes) |
| **45-degree** | Most versatile, natural dining view | Flat items with no height |
| **Straight-on (0°)** | Layered cakes, burgers, stacked items | Flat plates, spreads |
| **Slight low angle** | Heroic burger shots, towering desserts | Soups, flat dishes |
| **Close-up/Macro** | Texture details, ice cream scoops | Full table scenes |

## Food-Specific Template

```
[Shot type] of [dish with detailed description including texture words].
[Plating details and garnish]. Served [vessel: plate type, bowl, board].
[Props and surface description].
[Lighting: type + direction + quality].
[Camera: angle + lens + depth of field].
[Mood: color temperature + atmosphere].
```

## Examples by Food Category

### Hot Savory Dish
```
A close-up 45-degree shot of a steaming bowl of hand-pulled ramen. Rich,
cloudy tonkotsu broth with a glossy fat sheen. Topped with a jammy soft-boiled
egg cut in half revealing the golden yolk, two slices of caramelized chashu pork,
fresh scallions, and a sheet of nori. Wisps of steam rise from the bowl.
The ceramic bowl sits on a dark wooden counter. Soft side-light from the left
creates gentle shadows. Shot with an 85mm lens at f/2.8, shallow depth of field
blurring chopsticks resting beside the bowl. Warm, comforting atmosphere.
```

### Dessert
```
Overhead flat lay of a rustic galette on parchment paper. Golden-brown, flaky
pastry edges with caramelized sliced peaches arranged in a spiral. Light dusting
of powdered sugar catching the light. A scoop of vanilla bean ice cream beginning
to melt beside the galette. Scattered fresh thyme sprigs and a vintage pie server.
On a flour-dusted marble surface. Bright, airy natural light from above.
Shot with a 50mm lens, f/5.6 for moderate depth. Fresh, summery, pastoral mood.
```

### Beverage
```
A straight-on shot of an iced matcha latte in a clear glass. Layers clearly
visible: white oat milk at the bottom, vibrant green matcha in the middle,
a gentle gradient where they meet. Ice cubes catching the light with condensation
beading on the glass exterior. A bamboo matcha whisk and small ceramic bowl of
matcha powder beside the glass. On a light wooden surface.
Soft backlight creating a luminous glow through the glass. Shot at 85mm, f/2.0.
Clean, minimalist, zen-inspired composition with generous negative space.
```

### Breakfast / Brunch
```
A 45-degree shot of a complete French breakfast spread on a marble bistro table.
A golden-brown croissant torn open revealing airy, layered interior. A small pot
of strawberry confiture, a pat of butter on a ceramic dish, and a café crème in
a wide cup with a perfect heart of latte art. Crumbs scattered naturally on the
marble. Morning sunlight streaming from the right through a café window, casting
warm dappled shadows. Shot at 35mm to capture the full spread, f/4 for context.
Warm, Parisian morning atmosphere.
```

### Flat Lay / Social Media
```
Overhead flat lay of a loaded brunch table. Center: a stack of fluffy pancakes
with maple syrup dripping down the sides and a pat of melting butter on top.
Surrounding: a bowl of mixed berries, a glass of fresh orange juice, crispy
bacon strips on a small plate, a cup of black coffee, a folded linen napkin,
and scattered blueberries. On a warm wooden table.
Even, diffused overhead light, no harsh shadows. Shot at 35mm, f/5.6.
Bright, colorful, Instagram-worthy composition with every element intentional.
```

## Pro Tips for Food Prompts

1. **Motion sells**: "Syrup drizzling", "cheese stretching", "crumbs falling" — dynamic > static
2. **Imperfection is authentic**: "A few natural crumbs", "slightly rumpled napkin"
3. **Temperature cues**: Steam, condensation, melting — these signal freshness
4. **Color contrast**: "Bright green basil against deep red tomato sauce"
5. **Human element**: "A hand reaching for a slice" or "mid-pour of sauce" adds life
6. **Name the vessel**: "rustic ceramic bowl" vs "bowl" — specificity matters
7. **Seasonal context**: "Autumn squash soup" evokes different styling than "summer gazpacho"
8. **Less is more on garnish**: One perfect basil leaf > a pile of random herbs
